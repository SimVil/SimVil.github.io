Short Bio

I am an associate professor at Polytech Marseille within Aix-Marseille University. I am a member of the LIRICA team, at the LIS laboratory. Prior to that, I did a first postdoc at the LIRIS (INSA Lyon, Lyon, France), and a second one at the LORIA (Université de Lorraine, Nancy, France). I defended my PhD at the Université Clermont Auvergne in December 2021. My research mainly focuses on (enumeration) algorithms on closure systems and their representations.

Research links: Google Scholar, DBLP, Orcid
Keywords: closure systems, implications, meet-irreducible elements, enumeration.
